About the Museum

  • Founder: Abilkhan Kasteev – a prominent Kazakh artist
  • Location: Almaty, Kazakhstan
  • Established: 1935
  • Collection: Over 25,000 artworks, including traditional Kazakh art and international pieces
Click to expand and discover more about the museum!

The Kasteev Museum is not only a haven for art enthusiasts but also a cultural hub hosting exhibitions, lectures, and workshops. Its unique collection showcases the spirit and history of Kazakhstan, making it a must-visit destination.
