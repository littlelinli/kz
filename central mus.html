About the Museum

  1. Founder: Established under the guidance of the government of Kazakhstan
  2. Location: Almaty, Kazakhstan
  3. Established: 1931
  4. Collection: Over 300,000 artifacts, including archaeological finds, ethnographic items, and historical manuscripts
Click to expand and discover more about the museum!

The Central State Museum is a cornerstone of Kazakhstan’s cultural heritage. Its exhibits range from prehistoric artifacts to modern historical records, providing visitors with a comprehensive understanding of the nation's journey through time.
